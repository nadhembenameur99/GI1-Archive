FIFA 20 : Official Volta Gameplay

Wednesday, June 17, 2020
Go back to the streets with the authentic culture, creativity, and style of the small-sided game.

Your player
You can build your player, pick your gear, and express your style on the streets. From height and facial features to tattoos and hairstyles. Get the traditional athletic look, deck yourself out with the latest streetwear, or break boundaries as you find yours and your squad’s perfect style. Complete matches to increase your player’s rating and earn skill points. Max out various traits to earn titles that show off your gameplay personality.

Playgrounds
Show off your skills and experience a new side of The World’s Game in 17 football playgrounds from across the globe. Amsterdam, Paris, Tokyo, Barcelona, London, Cape Town and more.

Gameplay
Play various forms of small-sided football: 3v3 Rush (No GK’s), 4v4, 4v4 Rush, 5v5 and Professional Futsal, each with specific rules, formations and play styles.

Volta Story
Take your Avatar across the world and face off against various real-world legends of street football. Build your squad and recruit players as you aim to win the ultimate prize: The VOLTA FOOTBALL World Championship in Buenos Aires.
